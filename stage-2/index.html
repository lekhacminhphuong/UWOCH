    Our Mission

    Finding where to live on campus can be a difficult thing to decide. We have gone through the same process and we understand the struggle. Therefore, we would like to help you find the perfect housing the best way we know how. This website is a tool to help you find your future home at UW based on your preferences. With this webite, we hope to help freshman and transfer students or anyone who are interested in living on campus a step closer to find a perfect housing!
